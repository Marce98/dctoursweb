Federal Certifications 

We hold the following US Federal Government certifications: Veteran Owned Small Business, SBA 8(a), SDDD MBE and GSA Schedule 48 / SIN 411.1 Contract # GS-33F- 021AA. 


Local Certifications 

We hold the following local and regional government certifications: WMATA SBE, DC CBE & DBE, and PG MBE. 


We Take Safety Seriously 

DC Tours & Transportation maintains a fleet of safe and operationally sound vehicles most of which are less than two (2) years old. All of our transportation vehicles are ADA compliant and fully equipped with state-or-the-art technology. DC Tours & Trasnportation is proud to report zero incidents in all of our 20 years of service. We have not had any accidents that resulted in services disruption or injury. We attribute this record to our commitment to mandatory safety training for all drivers. 


Maintenance Schedule 

DC Tours & Transportation uses state of the art technology to ensure passenger safety, modern vehicles, real-time communications systems, and ADA compliant technologies and procedures. It is imperative that all drivers are proficient in the use and maintenance of such technologies. All of our capital investments in technology are accompanied by documented procedures and technical support to ensure equipment is maintained at operational readiness levels. The DC Tours & Transportation maintenance schedule includes daily operational checks, quarterly fluid, latch and emergency inspections as well as annual state inspections. Records are kept using a log to document bus maintenance. All drivers must deliver any roadside inspection reports and complete a daily post-trip inspection report.


Insurance and License 

DC Tours & Transportation insurance coverage exceeds the legal minimum required amount of insurance for actively deployed buses, which is determined by seating capacity. Additionally, carriers are required to obtain a license from the FMSCA to operate across state lines. Certificates of insurance can be provided either with our bid or immediately following contract award notice.
